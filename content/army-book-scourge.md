_All will serve._

## FACTION IDENTITY

**Theme:** Undeath incarnate. The Lich King's army of skeletons, ghouls, and necromancers. Relentless, inevitable, endless.

**Playstyle:** Swarm and attrition. Flood the board with cheap Undead that never test Morale. Necromancers raise fallen models as new Skeletons — the army grows as models die.

**Faction Mechanic — Raise Dead:** When any non-summoned model (friend or foe) is destroyed within 6" of a surviving Scourge model with the **Magical** keyword, the Scourge player may spend **1 Mana** to place a **Skeleton Warrior** at the destroyed model's position.

---

## HERO BODYGUARDS SYSTEM

Leaders may deploy with a protective screen of followers. While attached, the leader is limited to the **Movement** characteristic of the bodyguard models.

| Leader Type                | Bodyguard Unit       | Max Count | Special Rule                                                                            |
| :------------------------- | :------------------- | :-------- | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Lich (Hero)**            | **Acolyte**          | 3         | **Shatter Soul:** Destroy 1 bodyguard to negate all damage from a single non-Blast hit. |
| **Death Knight (Mounted)** | **Ghoul**            | 2         | **Loses Momentum:** Limited to Ghoul Movement.                                          |
| **Death Knight (On Foot)** | **Skeleton Warrior** | 2         | **Shatter Soul:** Used for survival or to fuel Level 3 abilities.                       |
| **Necromancer (Elite)**    | **Acolyte**          | 2         | **Shatter Soul:** Sacrificing Acolytes removes their passive Mana generation.           |

  - **Frozen Mechanic:** Targets must succeed a **Strength roll** vs the ability's STR; failing this, the unit is **Frozen** (Hold action only).
  - **Sacrifice:** Destroy 1 bodyguard to increase to **STR 7**.

## SPECIAL RULES

### Undead Army

- **Immune to Morale** — no Wavering, no Routing.
- **Cannot be healed** — destroyed is destroyed.
- **Immune to Taunt** — cannot be compelled.

### Living Scourge Units

**Necromancers** and **Acolytes** are living cultists. They test Morale normally and can be healed. They are the engine for Raise Dead.
